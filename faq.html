FAQs




    What is Electric Discussions (ED)?

ED is a community of individuals seeking to explore the depths of topics that affect and impact the average individual.


    What is a Revolution?

Revolutions are the number of times, each member has successfully presented their own topic.


    When do you have meetings?

We host meetings every Monday at 7:30pm. We do understand the idea of having a tight schedule. So, if you are presenting, we offer flexible scheduling between 7:30 pm and 9:30pm.


    Do I have to be a member to be able to attend meetings?

No you do not. You will be able to join all meetings. Becoming a member does allow you to contribute more in regards to growing and developing our community.


    How do I become a team member?

Click or copy this link https://electricdiscussions.co.uk/contact.html to get in touch and declare your interest.


    How are topics selected?

Every member is given the opportunity to submit a topic prior to each new revolution. After which, everyone votes on their topic of choice. The topic with the highest votes gets hosted next meeting date, then remaining topics will be voted on the next week.


    What topics are discussed on ED?

Well, anything really. We want to hear you deliver on topics you are passionate about. The more niche the topic the better.


    Am I good enough to present?

Our community will offer you all the support you need, and share their own experience on this journey of building online confidence


    If my presentation is bad, what will happen?

Immediately after every meeting, every participant will provide feedback, which will better prepare you for the next revolution.


    Will ED actually help me?

Most of our current community members have reported significant improvements in their eloquence and confidence levels.


    Do I have to pay anything?

There are NO joining or entrance fees.
