Electric Discussions is a society founded by a group of interlects; to nurture, inspire and encourage each other and also having positive impact on the lives of young adults at large, they strive to do this is respect, tolerance and good morals.

Learning should be free, enjoyable, and inspiring. Therefore, our community resources and content are free. We seek to inspire change in individuals’ hearts and minds through the beautiful use of personal anecdotes, by individuals from diverse backgrounds. Factual content is also incorporated, while bearing a humorous and uplifting vibe. All our content deliverers must start from somewhere. This is the perfect platform to, learn how to speak confidently. You will also practise using your voice to convey your meaning eloquently and lucidly.

All our meetings will be recorded and immediately shared with you, along with feedback from all the meeting attendees which allows you to immediately pick up on your strengths and weaknesses. Join one of our meetings and share your story with us.
